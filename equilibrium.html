Why It Works

The Science Behind It

Dermatology research consistently shows that over-cleansing combination skin drives more oil production — not less. The Equilibrium Bar is built around the opposite principle: mild cleansing paired with strong barrier support.

Mild Cleansing (16) — Safe for Daily Use

A low cleansing value removes surface impurities and T-zone oil without pushing the skin into defensive oil overproduction — the trap most harsh cleansers set.

High Conditioning (54) — No Dry Zones

A conditioning value well above average ensures dry cheek areas don't tighten after washing. The skin barrier stays intact where it's most fragile.

8% Superfat — Barrier Insurance

Extra unbound oils remain in the finished bar to nourish the skin's natural lipid layer — protecting against dehydration, irritation, and microbial invasion without clogging pores.

Balanced Hardness (43) — Face & Body Friendly

Not too hard, not too soft. A moderate hardness profile makes this bar gentle enough for daily facial use while durable enough for the whole body.

How to Use

Your Daily Ritual

1
Wet Your Skin

Rinse with lukewarm water. Avoid hot water — it opens pores aggressively and worsens dryness in already-dry zones.

2
Build a Lather

Work the bar into a smooth lather between palms, then apply across the face and body — including the T-zone, cheeks, and any combination areas.

3
Massage Gently

Use light circular motions for 30–60 seconds. Let the lather dwell briefly — the active ingredients need contact time to work across all zones.

4
Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse with cool water to close pores. Pat dry — never rub, especially on the drier areas of your face.

5
Store It Dry

A draining soap dish between uses keeps the bar firm, hygienic, and long-lasting.
