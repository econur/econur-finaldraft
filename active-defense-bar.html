Why It Works

The Science Behind It

According to dermatology guidance, effective cleansing is only beneficial when it reduces microbial load without compromising the skin barrier. This bar was designed to walk that line precisely.

Sebum & Sweat Removal

Coconut oil fraction provides the cleansing boost needed to cut through sweat and excess oil — while olive oil keeps the formula from going too harsh.

Pollution & Pore Debris

Activated charcoal binds to pollution particles and surface impurities through adsorption — physically drawing them off the skin without irritation.

Neem compounds and the essential oil blend target bacteria responsible for body odour — not just masking it, but reducing the microbial activity that causes it.

Castor oil's ricinoleic acid improves skin conditioning and prevents the excessive dryness that typically follows high-cleansing washes — keeping the barrier intact.

How to Use

Your Post-Workout Ritual

1
Rinse First

Rinse the skin with warm water to open pores and loosen surface sweat before applying the bar.

2
Build a Lather

Work the bar into a rich lather between palms or directly on skin. Focus on high-sweat zones — back, chest, underarms, and face.

3
Massage & Dwell

Spend 30–60 seconds working the lather in with circular motions. Let it sit briefly — charcoal and neem actives need contact time to do their job.

4
Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse with cool water to close pores and lock in the post-mint freshness. Pat dry — don't rub.

5
Store It Dry

A draining soap dish keeps the bar firm and hygienic between sessions — extending its life even with daily use.
